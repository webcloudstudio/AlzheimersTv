Overview

Multi Oscar winning musical; based on George Bernard Shaw's `Pygmalion'; about a phonetics expert who; for a bet; takes on the challenge of teaching a Cockney flower girl to talk like a duchess. All the songs from the stage musical were retained; including `The Rain in Spain; I Could Have Danced All Night; With a Little Bit of Luck; and Wouldn't It Be Loverly'.
